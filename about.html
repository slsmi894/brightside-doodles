About Us

Here at Bright Side Doodles, dogs have been an important part of my life for as long as I can remember.

When I was a toddler, I would carry my pup around with me everywhere I went. As a teen, I had two golden retreivers that carried me from high school to college and into my first career.

When those sweet red boys passed, I let another beautiful golden named Dre inot my heart. During Covid lockdown, I decided to add another boy named Cargo. It was with Cargo that I realized what I truly wanted to do was bring the joy of dogs to others. During my search for Cargo, I realized how hard it is to find a reputable breeder that cares for their dogs and treats them as family.

I am combining my love of animals and my medical knowledge in order to have the best breeding program.

Bright Side's dogs are all from strong family lines with health testing. These dogs are my heart and will be treated as such. Bright Side Doodles will bring you the joy that comes with owning a dog.
